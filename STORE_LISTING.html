Detailed Description

ProfiluxAI is an AI-powered LinkedIn profile optimizer that analyzes your profile across 5 key sections and gives you actionable recommendations to stand out to recruiters and connections.


5-Section Analysis: Your profile is scored across Headline, Summary, Experience, Skills, and Keywords — each worth 20 points for a comprehensive 100-point total score.


Visual Analytics: Beautiful animated score ring and radar chart show your strengths and weaknesses at a glance.


Headline Generator: Generate 10 alternative headlines with tone labels (Professional, Creative, Executive, Casual) and impact scores to find the perfect headline.


Summary Writer: AI writes your LinkedIn summary in 3 tones — Professional, Creative, or Executive — tailored to your experience and goals.


Auto-Extract: One-click profile extraction directly from LinkedIn. No copy-paste needed.


History & Export: Save up to 20 analysis results and export as Markdown, HTML, TXT, or PDF.
